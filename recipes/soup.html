Description

Dive into a bowl of warmth and comfort with our Hearty Vegetable Soup. This recipe brings together a medley of fresh, colorful vegetables simmered slowly to perfection in a savory broth. Each spoonful is a burst of flavors, combining the earthiness of carrots, the sweetness of tomatoes, and the robustness of celery. Infused with herbs like thyme and bay leaves, this soup is a hug in a bowl, perfect for cozy evenings or as a nutritious start to your meals.

Ingredients

  • one carrot
  • another carrot
  • less carrots, too much

Steps

  1. boil em
  2. mash em
  3. stick em in your soup
