Elevate your pasta night with this Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta. Al dente pasta is tossed in a rich, silky sauce made from garlic-infused cream and a generous helping of Parmesan cheese, resulting in a dish that's irresistibly creamy with a bold garlic punch. A sprinkle of freshly chopped parsley adds a touch of color and freshness, making this dish as pleasing to the eye as it is to the palate. Simple, elegant, and incredibly delicious, it's the perfect recipe for a romantic dinner or a comforting meal any day of the week.
